Media & Sponsorship

Adventis connects brands with audiences through strategic, multi-channel exposure across television, radio, print, events and influencer partnerships. We design campaigns that do more than reach people; they build recognition, trust and measurable growth.

Each project is planned to deliver real brand value. From a broadcast ad to a talent collaboration, everything we execute has a clear purpose: to strengthen visibility and inspire action.

Influencer marketing has become one of the most credible ways to reach people. At Adventis, we connect brands with creators who drive engagement and build authentic relationships with their audiences.

Our team manages every stage of collaboration, from selection and briefing to content review and reporting. We combine creativity with clear data tracking so brands can see the direct impact of each partnership.

  • Database of verified creators across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and X
  • End-to-end management of outreach, contracts, content direction and approvals
  • Audience quality checks and brand safety validation
  • Integrated reporting with UTM tracking and performance metrics
  • Long-term brand ambassador programs for consistent visibility

Television remains one of the most powerful channels for mass awareness. We secure prime-time visibility and sponsorships that combine creative storytelling with measurable results.

  • Audience-based channel mapping and planning
  • Custom sponsorships and integrations
  • Brand lift and GRP performance reporting

Radio builds repetition and emotion through voice and consistency. We partner with leading presenters and stations to position brands in moments of attention and connection.

  • Coverage across regional and national stations
  • Host-read mentions and integrations
  • Optimized scheduling during listener peaks

Print media remains a premium space for credibility and design impact. We use creative layouts and publication partnerships to deliver strong brand presence and trust.

  • Strategic placement in leading newspapers and magazines
  • Advertorial collaborations and editorial features
  • Creative layout support for visual consistency

We help brands connect with people in real life through experiences that create memories. From cultural events to sports and corporate sponsorships, we ensure every activation adds long-term value.

  • Sports, lifestyle and cultural partnerships
  • On-site activations and hospitality experiences
  • Cross-media amplification combining digital and physical exposure
